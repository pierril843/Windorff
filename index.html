About Us

We are located about 5 km north of Hanover in Grey County. It is a 2 hr drive from Toronto, London, or Kitchener. We are a small kennel with the dogs living in our home with us. I believe that Rottweiler's have the ability to be a great member of a family.

I live on a 50 acre wooded property with my 2 sons ; Riley and Maxwell , the Rotties and a Jack Russell named Lady.

We received our first Rottweiler as a rescue in 1986. She had been taken to the veterinarian to be euthanized but we were fortunate enough to save her. She quickly became a part of our family and convinced me there was no other breed for me.

We then acquired our first male "Fasen Vom Wuttenberg" from a friend and from then our involvement with Rottweilers has flourished.
