About Us

Welcome to Wendorff Registered Rottweilers, a small kennel owned and operated by Jacquelin. We are located about 5 km north of Hanover in Grey County, Ontario, on a 50 acre wooded property.

We specialize in breeding show-quality Rottweilers, and have had great success in both national and international competitions. Our dogs have won numerous awards and accolades

I (Jacquelin) have always believed that Rottweilers are great family members and a family oriented breed. We received our first Rottweiler, a rescue, in 1986 and she quickly became a part of our family. This experience convinced Jacquelin that there was no other breed for her, and we have been breeding and raising Rottweilers ever since.

At Wendorff Registered Rottweilers, we are committed to breeding and raising healthy, happy Rottweilers. We have 2-3 litters a year, and are currently accepting non-refundable deposits of $325 to reserve a puppy. The total price for a puppy is $2825; $2500, plus deposit.

If you are interested in one of our puppies, we encourage you to complete the online questionnaire on our website before contacting us. We also encourage you to come and meet the dogs and Jacquelin in person before submitting any deposit. This is a great opportunity to get to know the puppies and ensure that they are the right fit for your family.

For more information, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to helping you find the perfect Rottweiler companion!
